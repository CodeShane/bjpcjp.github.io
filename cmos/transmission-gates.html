Intro

The transmission gate (TG) is used in digital CMOS circuit design to pass or not pass a signal. It is built with the parallel connection of an NMOS and a PMOS device. The input-output resistance is defined as Rn||Rp.



Propagation delay time:
t_phl = t_plh = 0.7 . (Rn||Rp) . Cload
