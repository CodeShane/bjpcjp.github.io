--- title: Do you know how to "read" a face? layout: post tags: behavior date: 2020-08-01 ---

If you've read Malcolm Gladwell's "Blink", you know how human behaviors are often instinctive - to the point that you can't control your "tell" (to use a poker term).

Gladwell describes an ongoing study (Coan & Gottman) that breaks down human facial expressions (visual & spoken) into emotional states. It's not an easy technique to master - but Gottman can predict, with uncanny accuracy, whether a couple will be divorced with 5 years after listening to a few snips of conversation.

A descripition of can be downloaded at researchgate.net. I highly recommend it.
